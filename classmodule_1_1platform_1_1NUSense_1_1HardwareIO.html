enum module::platform::NUSense::HardwareIO::ServiceState
strongprivate

Tracks the service state of the reactor.

Enumerator
INIT 

The reactor is in the initial state from boot, waiting for a handshake response from NUSense.

IN_SERVICE 

The state of HardwareIO when it allows processing of ServoTargets messages. This state is sent to NUSense for reconnection purposes.
